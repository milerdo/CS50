How to play

1

Play 5 games a day

Each day you get 5 rounds. Guess a hidden number between 0 and 999 using 6 tries. Hot/warm/cold clues + higher/lower after each guess.

2

Guess early, earn more

1st try = 500 pts. The reward drops steeply each try. A 6th-try win earns 5 pts. Every guess still earns +1 pt.

3

Unlock the daily draw

After all 5 games, submit one lucky number (0–999) for the noon draw and one for the evening draw. Exact match = jackpot: +1000 pts!

4

Pick is locked, no changes

Once you submit your draw number it's final. Commitment creates anticipation — that's the fun!

5

Three leaderboards

Daily resets at midnight. Weekly resets Monday. All-time is forever. New players can top any board from day one.

FAQ

Best strategy?

Binary search: start at 500. Higher? Try 750. Lower? Try 250. Halve the range each time. With 6 tries you win ~50–60% using this method.

Draw odds?

Exactly 1 in 1000 (0.1%) per draw. Two draws a day = two chances. Pure luck — even beginners can hit it!

How do ranks work?

Based on all-time score: Newcomer → Guesser (100) → Sharp Eye (300) → Hot Shot (700) → Legend (1500) → Champion (3000).

Is it free?

100% free. No ads, no paywalls. Just numbers and good vibes.
