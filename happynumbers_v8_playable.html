How to play

1

Play 5 games a day

Each day you get 5 rounds. A hidden number 0–999 is chosen. Use the slider to pick your guess and hit Guess. You get 7 tries per round.

2

Read the clues

After each guess you get hot/warm/cold (within 50/150/further) plus higher/lower. Use them to zero in fast.

3

Guess early, earn big

1st try = 500 pts. The reward drops steeply each try. 7th try = 5 pts. Every guess earns +1 pt.

4

3 jackpot draws a day

After 5 games, unlock draw picks for 08:00, 16:00, and 00:00 draws. Pick one number per draw. Exact match = +2500 pts jackpot! Your pick locks immediately — no changes.

5

Three leaderboards

Daily resets at midnight. Weekly resets Monday. All-time is forever. New players can top any board from day one.

FAQ

Best strategy?

Binary search: start at 500. Hot and higher? Try 750. Cold and lower? Try 250. Each guess halves the range. With 7 tries you'll win roughly 60% using this method.

Draw odds?

Exactly 1 in 1000 (0.1%) per draw. Three draws a day = three chances. Pure luck — even a new player wins the jackpot!

How do ranks work?

Newcomer → Guesser (100) → Sharp Eye (300) → Hot Shot (700) → Legend (1500) → Champion (3000).

Is it free?

100% free. No ads, no paywalls.
